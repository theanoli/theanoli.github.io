## Why Open-Channel SSDs? 
* Applications want predictable performance
* Existing SSDs present too high-level of an abstraction to provide predictable performance
* Operators want good resource utilization
* Existing SSDs do not utilize resources effectively (i.e., you need more of a resource to do the same work)
